About the Association

Vistula-Sakura is a non-profit, voluntary networking organization connecting scientists, engineers, and professionals from Poland and Japan. We facilitate the exchange of knowledge, experience, and technology through academic and networking events.

Articles of Association (Summary)

Article 1-3: Purpose & Nature
  • Non-profit, voluntary networking with no legal personality or financial activity.
  • Promotes cooperation between Polish and Japanese researchers, universities, and industries.
  • Activities include annual conferences, seminars, and online discussions.
Article 4-7: Membership & Finances
  • Membership: Open and free to anyone interested in Polish‚ÄìJapanese cooperation. No fees.
  • Finances: The Association does not handle money or hold accounts. Financial support for events is provided directly by hosting institutions or sponsors.
  • Communication: This website serves as the primary official channel.
